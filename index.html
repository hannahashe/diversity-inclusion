Where to begin: practical tips

  • Start with a simple listening exercise or anonymous feedback.
  • Review your policies and documents with inclusive language in mind.
  • Make sure meetings are accessible: timing, format and materials.
  • Set a small, specific DEI goal for the next 3 months.
  • Share your intentions openly with staff, learners or volunteers.
  • Keep checking in and adjusting based on feedback and lived experience.
